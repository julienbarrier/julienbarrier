Hi, I'm Julien Barrier. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at ICFO - The
Institute of Photonic Sciences in Barcelona, Spain. My research involves quantum
transport in graphene heterostructures, combined with mid-IR and THz
excitations. In 2026, I will lead the [Low-Dimensional Electronics research
group](https://github.com/mpiLDE) at the Max Planck Institute for Solid State
Research.

My github profile includes projects I am working on as part of my academic
career, involving physics and lab tasks automation.
